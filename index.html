What is INEXON'26?

INEXON'26 is a Technical & Non-Technical Symposium organized by the Department of Master of Computer Applications (MCA) at Knowledge Institute of Technology, Salem. It's a platform for innovation, integration, and inspiration in the field of technology and computer applications.

The symposium brings together students, professionals, and tech enthusiasts to showcase their skills, exchange ideas, and collaborate on groundbreaking projects.
